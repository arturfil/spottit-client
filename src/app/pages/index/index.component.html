FAQ

BMR? MACROS? Don't worry it's all here.

What is BMR?

BMR stands for Basal Metabolic Rate, which is the amount of calories your body burns just by functioning.

What are Macros?

Macro-Nutrients or 'Macros' Are the Essential Nutrients that your body needs daily.

Why 'x' amount of carbs?

There is a formula that calculates the amount of grams of each Macro. It is calculated based on your unique stats, though it is advise to check your body stats weekly in order to adjust.

'I thought fats were bad'

Fats are an ESSENTIAL Macronutrient so no, as long as it comes form avocado, peanuts or unsaturated fats in general (NOT JUNKFOOD) you are fine.
